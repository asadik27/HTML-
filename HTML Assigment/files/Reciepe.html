Chicken Baryani Reciepe


Ingredients

  • 1 kilo Basmati Rice
  • 500g Chicken, cut into pieces
  • 200g sliced onians
  • 300g tomatos, choped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 4-5 cups water
  • 4-5 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves and mint leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and saut√© the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half of the onions and set them aside for garnishing.
  3. Add chicken pieces to the pot and cook until they turn white. Add ginger and garlic paste, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften.
  5. Stir in yogurt and cook for a couple of minutes.
  6. Add soaked rice and water to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Cover and cook until the rice and chicken are cooked through.
  7. Garnish with the reserved fried onions, fresh coriander leaves, and mint leaves.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Chicken Biryani!

Tips:

You can also add saffron milk on top of the biryani for an enhanced flavor and aroma.
